About

I’m a full stack developer with 5+ years of experience building web and mobile applications using React, TypeScript, and Firebase.

Recently, I’ve been focused on building products end-to-end — from idea to production — including mobile apps and platforms in the productivity and mental health space.

One of my projects, Tusk, started as a tool for my son (AuDHD) to help him capture tasks quickly without being overwhelmed by complex interfaces. That experience shaped how I think about product design — simple, low-friction, and user-first.

I enjoy working across the full stack: designing UI, building backend logic, integrating APIs, and shipping to production. I also actively use AI-assisted development tools to move faster and iterate on ideas.

I’m particularly interested in:

  • productivity and mental health products
  • low-friction UX and voice-first interfaces
  • AI-driven features

Currently open to remote opportunities where I can contribute to building meaningful, user-centered products.
